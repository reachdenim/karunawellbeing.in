About MindWell

At MindWell, we believe mental health is not a privilege—it's a right. Since 2015, our team of psychiatric social workers, neuro-psychiatrists, and clinical psychologists have been transforming lives with evidence-based care, innovative therapies, and a patient-first approach.

From humble beginnings to serving patients across 5+ countries, MindWell has touched over 10,000 lives with a remarkable 94% success rate. Guided by empathy, early intervention, and global accessibility, we're committed to breaking stigma and making healing possible for everyone.
